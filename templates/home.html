About

UPI Fraud Shield is an AI/ML-based fraud detection system that helps identify suspicious UPI transactions and protects users from online payment scams.

The system analyzes:

  • Sender and Receiver UPI IDs
  • Transaction Amount
  • Device Activity
  • Transaction Frequency
  • Payment Behavior

to predict whether a transaction is safe or fraudulent.
